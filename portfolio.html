KONTROLA POD PRITISKOM

CONTROL UNDER PRESSURE

Ovi radovi ne prikazuju sistem kao stabilan.
Oni prikazuju trenutak u kojem stabilnost počinje da se troši.

Forma ostaje prisutna čak i kada gubi funkciju.
Struktura ne nestaje — ona se deformiše.

Svaki rad je stanje između kontrole i njenog raspada.
Ne postoji završetak, samo promena pritiska.

Ovo nije pregled sistema.
Ovo je sistem u trenutku kada više ne može da se vrati u mir.

These works do not present the system as stable.
They present the moment in which stability begins to erode.

Form remains present even as it loses function.
Structure does not disappear — it deforms.

Each work is a state between control and its collapse.
There is no ending, only a shift in pressure.

This is not an overview of a system.
This is the system at the moment it can no longer return to calm.

STATIČNA TENZIJA

STATIC TENSION

Forma ostaje stabilna.

Ne zato što je sigurna, već zato što još nije narušena.
Sistem deluje zatvoreno, ali u sebi već nosi pritisak koji se ne oslobađa.

Ništa se ne pomera — ali ništa nije mirno.

Ovi radovi predstavljaju početnu tačku kontrole pre njenog pomeranja.

Form remains stable.

Not because it is secure, but because it has not yet been disrupted.
The system appears closed, but already carries pressure that is not released.

Nothing moves — yet nothing is still.

These works represent the initial state of control before displacement begins.

ODSTUPANJE KONTROLE

CONTROL DRIFT

Sistem više nije stabilan, ali još uvek funkcioniše.

Kontrola se pomera, bez potpunog prekida.
Elementi izlaze iz poravnanja, ali struktura i dalje drži formu.

Greška postaje deo procesa, a ne njegov kraj.

Ovo je trenutak kada stabilnost počinje da gubi jasnoću.

The system is no longer stable, but still functions.

Control shifts without fully collapsing.
Elements drift out of alignment, yet structure still holds form.

Error becomes part of the process, not its end.

This is the moment stability begins to lose clarity.

KOLAPS STRUKTURE

STRUCTURE FAILURE

Struktura ostaje prisutna, ali gubi funkciju.

Forma više ne podržava sistem koji je izgradila.
Napetost prelazi u lom, a stabilnost postaje privid.

Ono što je održavalo red sada ga razara iznutra.

Ovo je tačka u kojoj sistem počinje da se ruši pod sopstvenom logikom.

Structure remains present, but loses function.

Form no longer supports the system it created.
Tension turns into rupture, and stability becomes illusion.

What once maintained order now destroys it from within.

This is the point where the system begins to collapse under its own logic.

PREOPTEREĆENJE

OVERLOAD

Signal postaje previše gust da bi se razložio.

Sistem ostaje aktivan, ali gubi mogućnost rasterećenja.
Informacija se gomila bez izlaza i bez prekida.

Kolaps se ne dešava naglo — on traje.

Ovo nije kraj sistema, već stanje u kojem on više ne može da prestane.

The signal becomes too dense to resolve.

The system remains active, but loses the ability to release.
Information accumulates without exit or interruption.

Collapse does not happen suddenly — it persists.

This is not the end of the system, but the state in which it can no longer stop.
